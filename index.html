Founder
Manuel Rodriguez has been a worshiping musician for many years. He has facilitated conferences for worship leaders and musicians and participated as a musician and keynote speaker. He has functioned in various leadership roles in the church that have allowed him to flow in his gifts as a leader, musician, and teacher. Manuel’s accomplishments include: completion of 10 years at The Worship Institute in Dallas, Texas, publication of the “The Worshiping Musician 30-day Devotional,” release of his own solo albums, and recordings on multiple artist’s albums. He is currently continuing his education in Music Recording Technology at Salt Lake Community college in the beautiful state of Utah.
